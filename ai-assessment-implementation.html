Music Production

AI-Assisted Composition Analysis

Task: Use AI to generate harmonic progressions, then create original melody and arrangement

Assessment Criteria:

  • Quality of prompts and AI direction (20%)
  • Musical understanding in melody creation (30%)
  • Professional arrangement decisions (30%)
  • Critical reflection on AI vs human contributions (20%)
Visual Arts

AI-Enhanced Concept Development

Task: Use AI for initial visual research, develop personal artistic interpretation

Assessment Criteria:

  • Depth of visual research and AI curation (25%)
  • Original conceptual development (35%)
  • Technical execution and material choices (25%)
  • Critical analysis of AI limitations in creative process (15%)
Film & Media

AI-Supported Script Development

Task: Use AI for character development ideas, write original dialogue and scenes

Assessment Criteria:

  • Strategic use of AI for character research (20%)
  • Original dialogue and narrative voice (40%)
  • Understanding of cinematic storytelling (25%)
  • Reflection on human vs AI creativity in writing (15%)

üîÑ Step 1: Identify What Needs Replacing

Assessment Types to Replace ‚ñº
  • Information processing tasks: Where the main challenge is organizing and presenting existing information
  • Compliance-dependent assignments: That rely entirely on students following "don't use AI" rules
  • Administrative convenience tasks: Designed for easy grading rather than learning goals
  • Generic research papers: That AI can complete with basic prompting
  • Standard format assignments: Where format matters more than thinking
Structural Replacement Strategies ‚ñº

Product ‚Üí Process

From: Submit final essay

To: Portfolio of development stages

Written ‚Üí Interactive

From: Written report

To: Presentation + live Q&A

Generic ‚Üí Personal

From: Research any topic

To: Analyze work in your local context

Individual ‚Üí Collaborative

From: Solo assignment

To: Group work with individual accountability

Combined Approach: Process + AI Integration

‚Üê Back to Overview

Document the learning journey while allowing thoughtful AI use

üîó Step 1: Design the Integration

What This Approach Includes ‚ñº
  • Process documentation showing development stages and decision-making
  • AI conversation logs demonstrating how students directed and critiqued AI
  • Reflection on both human creativity and AI collaboration
  • Assessment of subject knowledge throughout the entire journey
Implementation Framework ‚ñº

Phase 1: Initial Development (Weeks 1-3)

  • Initial brainstorming and concept maps (with or without AI)
  • AI exploration log if used
  • Personal reflection on ideas generated

Phase 2: Research & Development (Weeks 4-6)

  • Research log showing AI searches and human verification
  • Documentation of how AI suggestions were evaluated
  • Evidence of discipline-specific knowledge applied

Phase 3: Creation & Iteration (Weeks 7-9)

  • Drafts/prototypes with annotations about AI suggestions accepted/rejected
  • Documentation of creative decisions and iterations
  • Peer feedback integration process

Phase 4: Completion & Reflection (Weeks 10-12)

  • Final product with critical commentary on the process
  • Reflection on what they learned about both the topic and working with AI
  • Professional implications and future applications
